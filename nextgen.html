Why it matters

What an internship is actually for

Not a credential to collect — a low-stakes place to find out whether the work matches the idea of it in your head, before a job asks you to find out the hard way.

Applied practice

Coursework teaches concepts in isolation. An internship forces you to combine them against a task that doesn't care which chapter you're on.

Evidence, not adjectives

"Detail-oriented" on a resume convinces no one. A shipped project with your name on the commit history does the convincing for you.

A lower-cost mistake

Finding out you dislike a field during a four-week internship is cheap. Finding out during a four-year career is not.

The process

Four stamps, in order

The sequence matters — each stamp is only granted once the last one is checked off.

02

Take tasks

01. Register & Onboard

Immediate

Kickstart your journey. There are no gating interviews or applications; select a track and enter your info to immediately download your Intern Passport card.

  • Choose from any of our 31 domain tracks.
  • Download and secure your passport ledger entry.
  • Get immediate access to task sheets & files.
"Zero friction onboarding. I had my task guidelines in less than two minutes."

02. Attempt Curriculum Tasks

Self-paced

Every track features tasks sorted from introductory to advanced complexity. Code, compile, or write in an environment of your choosing—we supply guidelines, you supply the code.

  • Work strictly at your own schedule.
  • Build real-world portfolios with production setups.
  • Skip tasks that don't match your skill interest levels.
"Tasks are scoped to mirror actual junior engineer onboarding stories."

03. Code Submission & Review

48 Hour Turnaround

Submit your GitHub repository links accompanied by documentation detailing your design solutions. A domain mentor reviews code compliance against task requirements.

  • Mentorship reviews matching code quality & logic.
  • Receive actionable revision notes if corrections are needed.
  • Passport status changes to 'Verified' upon passing.
"Feedback is detailed. It's not just pass/fail—they check how you structured queries."

04. Verification & Certification

Cryptographically Signed

Upon review approval of your tasks, a secure certificate registry ID is issued. Any hiring manager can immediately verify your credentials against the public directory.

  • Unique checkable ledger ID appended to your credentials.
  • Embed directly to LinkedIn, PDF resumes, or personal portfolios.
  • Public validation details lists actual deliverables.
"Managers love the fact they can verify the repository link on the certificate."
